Instant Pot Thai Noodles with Chicken

Description

This is a fantastic Thai peanut noodle with chicken recipe, all made in the instant pot. The original thai noodle recipe comes from 365daysofcrockpot.com.

I love this recipe because it is quick and easy, but tastes great! Everything is cooked right in the instant pot which makes for easy clean up. Not to mention the instant pot is great for nights when people are coming and going because once it is finished cooking, you can set it on warm to keep it warm for 2 hours!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1.5lb chicken breast or thigh
  • 3 clove minced garlic
  • 2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 8 oz whole wheat spaghetti noodles
  • Lime juice
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • Chopped Peanuts for topping

Steps

  1. Turn on pressure cooker to saute mode and let heat up
  2. While heating up, cut up the chicken into cubes. Also cut up the bell pepper into small chunks
  3. Once hot, put oil in pressure cooker. Then put in chopped chicken. And saute for ~3 minutes
  4. Add in garlic and saute for 30 seconds
  5. Add in chicken broth and scrape bottom of pressure cooker to get any burnt pieces up off the bottom
  6. Add in soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and bell pepper
  7. Break noodles in half and push down into pressure cooker. Liquid should just cover the noodles
  8. Cover pressure cooker and cook on manual mode for 3-5 minutes
  9. Release steam valve and open top
  10. Stir in peanut butter and lime rice. Plate up and top with crushed peanuts
